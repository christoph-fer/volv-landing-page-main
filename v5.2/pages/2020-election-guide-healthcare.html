Healthcare



Healthcare has always been a key election issue especially with incumbent decisions regarding COVID-19, abortion, and Obamacare.



President Donald Trump

Supports free COVID-19 testing

  • The Trump administration has faced significant backlash for its delayed COVID-19 response
  • Made COVID-19 testing free
  • Has made repeated calls to reduce COVID-19 testing, and made comparisons of the disease with the flu - both of which have been criticized by healthcare experts
  • Promised to develop a COVID-19 vaccine by the end of 2020


Does not support Obamacare

  • Asked the Supreme Court to repeal the Affordable Care Act
  • Rolled back the individual mandate which was part of the ACA, eliminating the tax penalty for not having health coverage.
  • 2018: The Trump administration offered Americans cheaper one-year policies, under which insurers can charge higher premiums or deny coverage based on medical history
  • In the last Presidential debate, Trump promised to come up with a new plan that would cover people with pre-existing health conditions.


Does not support the WHO
